What is ZED Registration?

The Zero Defect Zero Effect (ZED) Certification is a Government of India initiative aimed at empowering MSMEs to enhance their competitiveness, sustainability, and global reach. By adopting ZED practices, MSMEs can significantly reduce waste, improve productivity, save energy, and embrace environmentally conscious operations.

This certification encourages best practices in work culture, product standardization, and process optimization, transforming MSMEs into National and International Champions. Beyond certification, ZED provides a comprehensive framework of assessment, guidance, and technological intervention to strengthen the MSME ecosystem and drive their growth.

Benefits of ZED

  • Process Improvement: Enhances internal management and operational systems, leading to increased revenues and profitability.
  • Global Competitiveness: Builds a credible vendor database to strengthen global market opportunities.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Reduces negative environmental impact through sustainable practices.
  • Recognition and Rewards: Provides recognition through awards and other incentives.
  • Adoption of Best Practices: Aligns MSMEs with global standards and best practices.

Documents Required for ZED Registration

  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Self-Assessment Report
  • Financial Statements
  • Quality Management System (QMS) Documentation
  • Environmental Management System (EMS) Documentation
  • Energy Management System (EnMS) Documentation
  • Safety Management Documentation
  • Human Resource Management Documentation
  • Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Documentation
  • Design Management Documentation
