  1. What is Digital Marketing?
    • Digital marketing refers to the use of online platforms, tools, and strategies to promote products, services, or brands. Unlike traditional marketing, digital marketing leverages the internet and digital technologies to connect with audiences in a more targeted and measurable way. It includes tactics like search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, email marketing, and more.
