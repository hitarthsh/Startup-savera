Trademark Registration

Trademark registration in India is essential for safeguarding your business identity, including its name, logo, or symbol. It grants you exclusive legal rights to prevent unauthorized use or imitation of your identity hence protecting your brand’s uniqueness and reputation.

The process begins with a trademark search to confirm availability, followed by filing a trademark application with IP India under the Government of India. Registering a trademark secures your brand and adds value to your business in a competitive market.

  • Why Register Your Trademark?
    • Trademark registration in India secures your exclusive rights to use your brand name or logo for your goods or services. It provides strong protection against infringement, allowing you to take legal action against unauthorized use. Once registered, a trademark is valid for ten years from the filing date and can be renewed indefinitely, ensuring long-term brand security and recognition.

Who can apply for Trademark Registration

  • Individuals
  • Joint Owners
  • Proprietorship Firms
  • Partnership Firms (maximum of ten partners)
  • Limited Liability Partnerships (LLPs)
  • Indian Companies
  • Foreign Companies
  • Trusts
  • Societies

Documents Required

  • Trademark application form
  • Proof of applicant’s identity and address.
  • Logo or image of the trademark (if applicable).
  • Details of goods or services covered by the trademark.
  • Authorisation Letter or Power of Attorney.
