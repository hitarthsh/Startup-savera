Shram Suvidha

The Shram Suvidha Portal, launched by the Ministry of Labour & Employment, serves as a one-stop platform for all labor compliance needs. It simplifies application procedures, enhances transparency in inspections, and ensures swift grievance redressal. Acting as a single point of contact between employers, employees, and enforcement agencies, the portal is a key initiative under the ‘Ease of Doing Business’ labor law reforms, streamlining compliance and fostering efficiency in the labor ecosystem.

Benefits

Unified Information Platform

Labour Identification Number (LIN)

Online Notice Management

Employee Grievance Redressal

Services Offered by Shram Suvidha Portal

  • Management and updates for inspectable establishments, including inspection reports.
  • Online data entry by employers, establishments, and enforcement agencies.
  • Entity verification by enforcement agencies.
  • Labour Identification Number (LIN) generation.
  • Email/SMS notifications to establishments.
  • Pre-assigned user ID and password for users, with an option to change passwords.
  • Self-service login and password generation for inspectable establishments.
  • Initial stage LIN generation by CLC(C) organization.
  • Online submission of annual returns for CLC(C) and DGMS.
  • Common platform for EPFO and ESIC monthly return submissions.
  • Modification and verification of LIN data.
